How our scam detection engine works

ScamShield AI looks for three things in every message.

Urgency

Scams rush you. "Act now," "24 hours," "your account will be locked" — pressure is the first tell.

Ask

What does it actually want — a click, a code, a gift card, a wire transfer? Real organizations rarely ask this way.

Identity

Is the sender who they claim to be? Mismatched links, odd domains, and generic greetings give it away.
